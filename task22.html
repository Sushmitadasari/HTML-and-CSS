PRABHAS

Early Life

Prabhas, born on October 23, 1979, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is the son of film producer U. Suryanarayana Raju. He was raised in Hyderabad and completed his early education there. Although he came from a film background, Prabhas initially showed little interest in acting. Later, inspired by his family, he decided to enter the film industry.

Education

Prabhas completed his schooling in Hyderabad and earned a B.Tech degree. He then joined the Satyanand Film Institute in Visakhapatnam to pursue professional training in acting before making his debut in cinema.

Career in Cinema

Prabhas made his debut with the film Eeswar in 2002. He gained popularity with Varsham in 2004 and continued delivering hits like Chatrapathi (2005), Billa (2009), and Darling (2010). His major breakthrough came with the global blockbuster Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) and its sequel Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017), which brought him worldwide fame and recognition.

Filmography & Recognition

He has also starred in notable films such as Rebel (2012), Mirchi (2013), Saaho (2019), Radhe Shyam (2022), and Adipurush (2023). His upcoming project, Kalki 2898 AD (2024), is highly anticipated and is set to be a groundbreaking sci-fi movie in Indian cinema.

Prabhas is known for his humble nature and dedication to his craft. He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015 and is regarded as one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema.
